AoT Baked Potato.

Description.

Attack on Titan is as intruiging as it is disastrous. Through all the hardship, most of the top characters survived, until Sasha was shot by some dumb kid who got way too much screen time and for some reason is still alive. In Sasha's loving memory, we shall share a recipe for her first dish on the show and disdain for her murderer, whose name shall not be mentioned. Please enjoy and keep hating the little evil girl!

Ingredients.

  • Baking Sheets
  • Parchment Paper
  • 4 medium russet potatoes
  • Extra Virgin Oil
  • Sea Salt

Steps.

  1. Preheat oven to 425 Farhenheit or 218 Celsius and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Use a fork to poke a few holes into the potatoes. Place them on the baking sheet, rub with olive oil and sprinkle sea salt all over.
  3. Bake 45 to 60 minutes or until potato is fork tender and skin is crisp
  4. Slice open each potato, fluff the insides and serce with desired topping
