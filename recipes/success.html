Saiki's Coffee Jelly.

Credit: Pintrest

Description.

Coffe Jelly is a Japanese Desert that transforms the deliciousness of coffee from a drink to a smooth, sweet, delicous jelly. Saiki's obsession with coffee jelly nurtured curiosity, later love, for it in me too. There are many variatins of the desert, all different but delicous. Please enjoy.

Ingredients.

  • 3 tspns instant coffee powder
  • 3 tbspns granulated sugar
  • 1.5 cups hot water
  • 2 envelopes unflavoured gelatin
  • Heavy cream or evaporated milk

Steps.

  1. In a heat safe bowl or measuring cup, dissolve instant coffee and sugar in hot water.
  2. Add gelatin to the coffee mixture and stir until gelatinis desolved. Let it sit for five minutes
  3. Cool to room temperature and pour intto individual molds or a large rectangular pan
  4. Refrigirate unti set,4-5hrs. Unmold unto individual plates or cut into cubes and serve in bowls.
  5. Garnish with a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of evaporated milk and enjoy.
